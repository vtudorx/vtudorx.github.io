I am a System Architect working across AWS cloud architecture, infrastructure automation, and software systems that are practical to operate and change. Most of my work sits at the point where business goals need to become technical decisions: what to build, how to run it, how to secure it, and how to keep delivery moving.

My main stack is Go, PHP/Symfony, Python, and AWS CDK. On AWS, I work with services such as EKS, Lambda, ECS/Fargate, Aurora, DynamoDB, EventBridge, S3, Transit Gateway, IAM, and Cognito.

Recently, I have been building AI-enabled business intelligence using AWS Bedrock, Claude, Tableau, and custom MCP sidecars. The goal is simple: help stakeholders ask business questions in natural language and get useful answers from data they already have.

My background combines hands-on engineering with technical leadership. I often start by building proof-of-concepts around unclear problems, then help turn the useful ones into sustainable solutions. That usually means making the domain model explicit, choosing modular boundaries carefully, keeping infrastructure costs visible, and documenting decisions through ADRs, shared standards, and engineering workshops.
